The sign
of a good time.

Bask in the warm Da Nang evenings under the glow of the street lights on our patio. Pull up a chair, sip on a signature cocktail mixed by our talented bartenders, and settle in — you’re exactly where you’re meant to be.

Join the Action
at the Patio

Grab a seat closer to the action! Whether you're here for a laid-back Lady’s Night, a brainy Trivia challenge, or one of our famous Cocktail Masterclasses, there’s always something happening at the Patio. Check our events page for what’s coming up next!

Sometimes we close our doors to the public
and host private functions

Looking for a space that’s all yours? We love hosting birthdays, team events, and special celebrations. With the whole Patio reserved just for you, it’s the perfect spot to create unforgettable moments by the beach.

21 Sundays

Start your week the Patio way — relaxed, sun-soaked, and full of flavor. Keep the weekend spirit alive with good food, craft drinks, and fresh ocean air right here in Da Nang.

  • Mixology Mondays

    Every first Monday, join our Mixology Masters on the patio as they teach you how to craft stunning cocktails using local ingredients and international techniques. Sip, shake, and show off at your next gathering!

  • It's 5 o'clock Sommelier

    It’s always wine time at Patio! Pop by for our casual wine workshops hosted by visiting sommeliers — a perfect way to explore new flavors from Vietnam and beyond.

  • California Dreamin'

    Dreaming of the West Coast? Savor the best of California wines paired with small bites, beachside vibes, and the sound of the surf. From zesty Sauvignon Blancs to rich Napa Cabs, there’s a pour waiting for you.

  • The Sounds of Sunset

    Every last Wednesday, the patio transforms into a live music haven. Enjoy laid-back sets from Da Nang’s rising stars as the sun dips into the sea. Good tunes, cold drinks, and great company guaranteed.

  • Drink for a Cause

    Every weeknight, order our “Patio Passion” special and we’ll donate 100% of profits to local Da Nang charities supporting children and families. Cheers to making a difference, one drink at a time!

  • Host Your Own Event

    Got an idea for a night to remember? Whether it’s a pop-up market, beach party, or special dinner, our team loves bringing new ideas to life. Talk to us about hosting your next event at Patio!

Would you like to get involved?

You can always ask our lovely staff about upcoming events and promotions or about hosting your own private event, but if you feel more comfortable asking online that is also wonderful.
