21 Sundays

Come and day drink with us while we celebrate the beauty of life. Can't let the Monday Blues get you down if you are still up from Sunday!

  • Cocktail Master Class

    Every first Monday of the month we have a Cocktail master class where you can come and learn from the industry's best how to prepare a beautiful cocktail at home and WOW your guests.

  • It's 5 o'clock Sommelier

    It is always 5 o'clock somewhere, so come join our professional sommelier to learn more about your favorite wines.

  • California Dreamin'

    Have you been dreaming of California? Come and sample some of our California wine colection from crisp Sauv Blancs to deep merlots. With so many optoins you are sure to find something to match your palette.

  • The Sounds of Spirits

    Every last Wednesday of the week we host our very own "Queen of the Spirits" who you can usually find behind the bar, but during The Sounds of Spirits Wednesdays she can be found behind the microphone accompanied by a small band of misfits.

  • Charity Night

    Nothing is better than drinking for a good cause. Every weeknight order the "Cat Butler Special" and 100% of profits from that drink will be donated to The Cat Butler Charity

  • Do you have an idea for an even?

    If you have an event that you would like to see hosted at our bar please inform our attentive staff. We are always open to new ideas and ways that we can serve the public and our customers.

Would you like to get involved?

You can always ask our lovely staff about upcoming events and promotions or about hosting your own private event, but if you feel more comfortable asking online that is also wonderful.
